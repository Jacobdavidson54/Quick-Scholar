About

QuickScholar was built by a student who experienced firsthand how much time is wasted searching for relevant academic papers and sources across disconnected and inefficient data platforms. What should be a focused research process often turns into hours of frustration, switching between tabs, broken sources, and unreliable search results. The aim of QuickScholar is simple: to create a smooth, fast, and unified research experience for students working on academic tasks. By bringing relevant papers and citations into one streamlined tool, QuickScholar helps students focus more on learning and thinking, and less on searching. It is designed with students in mind — built from a students perspective, for students everywhere.
